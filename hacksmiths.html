Peacock

Getting ready to present at #SexTechHack. Can you guess what it is? pic.twitter.com/jUDCUmEnHO

— Libby Odai (@LibbyOdai) December 18, 2016

I was lucky enough to be able to attend the Sex Tech Hack at Goldsmiths, a hackathon brining innovation to technology around intimacy, companionship and sexuality.

As well as messing around making 3D printed sex toy prototypes, I teamed up with Lisa Evelyn, Tom Chambers and Tom Gurion. Together we made Peacock a project to explore the bounds of gender, sexuality and performance. Our project ended up winnning the Best Performance Art prize.
