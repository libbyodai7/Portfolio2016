Commuter Cards

Travelling the long commute from Reading to London, I found myself asking how can I make this fun?

Commuter Cards is a small project I made to learn VB.net and C#.

Battle Mr. Mauve to nab his seat by choosing a number. Sometimes the highest number wins, sometimes the lowest, so pick carefully!
